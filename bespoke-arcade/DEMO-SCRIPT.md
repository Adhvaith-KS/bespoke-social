# Bespoke Social — Demo Video Script (3:00)

Narrator: Adhvaith, on camera for the open and close, voiceover elsewhere.
Tone: deadpan office comedy. Let the app do the visual work, keep cuts fast.
Anything in [brackets] is a visual or editing direction.

---

**[0:00 - 0:20] COLD OPEN**

[Adhvaith on camera, webcam framing, very serious]

ADHVAITH: Since we work across the world, Tarun, Shrey, and I really felt
like we needed a bespoke social lounge where we could all chill and get to
know each other. Introducing Stitch, our culture overlord, whose sole
purpose is to make sure everyone has fun. He lives inside Bespoke Social
and our Slack.

[Cut to the home page. The robotic goat bobs gently. Hold for a beat.]

ADHVAITH (V.O.): He is a robotic goat. We did not question it either.

---

**[0:20 - 0:35] THE LOGIN**

[Screen: login page, Google button]

ADHVAITH (V.O.): Security is airtight. You sign in with your bespokelabs
email, and only your bespokelabs email. My personal Gmail tried to get in
once. Stitch signed it out mid-click. No trial. No appeal.

---

**[0:35 - 1:00] BESPOKE WORDLE**

[Screen: typing a guess, tiles flip]

ADHVAITH (V.O.): Every day starts with Bespoke Wordle. Company vocabulary,
six guesses, and a dictionary with sixteen thousand real words. I want to
be clear about the word real, because for about a week this game accepted
literally anything.

[Screen: typing ZZXQJ, the board shakes, Not in the dictionary]

ADHVAITH (V.O.): Shrey averaged two guesses that week. Career week for
Shrey. We have since patched the dictionary and his ego.

[Screen: solve it, the Today's Wordle board slides in showing all three players]

ADHVAITH (V.O.): Finish and you instantly see who beat you. For context on
how that feels, here is Tarun beating me by two guesses before I had
coffee.

---

**[1:00 - 1:20] BESPOKLE**

[Screen: ladder solve, then the reveal: BES stamps in next to POKE, confetti]

ADHVAITH (V.O.): Bespokle is our word ladder. Change one letter at a time
until you reach POKE, and then. Well. Watch.

[Let the BESPOKE stamp and confetti play with sound. Say nothing. Beat.]

ADHVAITH (V.O.): We spent real engineering hours on that. Zero regrets.

---

**[1:20 - 1:40] TRIVIA**

[Screen: trivia timer counting down, pick an answer, reveal]

ADHVAITH (V.O.): Company trivia. Four questions, twenty seconds each, and
a speed bonus that has turned Tarun into something the leaderboard
describes as legal but concerning. The questions cover actual company
lore. Our open source library. Where the word bespoke comes from. What
the donut bot does. If you get that last one wrong we need to talk.

---

**[1:40 - 2:10] BE(SPOKE)REAL + TWO TRUTHS ONE LIE**

[Screen: BE(spoke)REAL feed with the weekend photos, tap a heart]

ADHVAITH (V.O.): BE(spoke)REAL. One photo prompt a day, post any time
before midnight, caption it yourself. Likes are anonymous, which means the
seven hearts on Shrey's plant photo could be anyone. It was me. All seven.

[Screen: Two Truths One Lie, Adhvaith's card with the three statements]

ADHVAITH (V.O.): Then there is Two Truths, One Lie. Today's victim is me.
One of these is false. I almost fell off a mountain, I went viral in a
suit at the Minions movie, and I watched the same film twice a day for a
week. Vote in the app. Results drop on Slack at end of day, so you get to
stew about it until then. That is the fun part. The stewing.

---

**[2:10 - 2:30] STORY, CARDS, DIGEST**

[Screen: story chain scroll, quick]

ADHVAITH (V.O.): The story chain. One person adds a few lines a day.
Current saga: a box of donuts nobody will touch because of a sticky note.
Day three. Tensions are high.

[Screen: cards page, silhouettes]

ADHVAITH (V.O.): Coffee chat cards. The donut bot pairs you in
#virtual-coffee, you actually have the chat, you mint their card. Working
on the same team does not count. Those are the rules. Stitch wrote them.

[Screen: weekly culture digest, scroll past the photo and the medal table]

ADHVAITH (V.O.): And every week Stitch writes up the whole thing in the
culture digest, including the best photo of the week and a leaderboard
table for the petty among us. So, me.

---

**[2:30 - 3:00] CLOSE**

[Screen: leaderboard, then cut back to Adhvaith on camera]

ADHVAITH: Everything you do here earns bespoke social points. Wordle,
trivia, photos, showing up. Being active is the whole meta. Right now
Tarun is first, I am second, and I have chosen to interpret that as
temporary.

[Screen: home page, Stitch bobbing. Text overlay: Bespoke Social]

ADHVAITH (V.O.): Bespoke Social. Built by three people in three time
zones, supervised loosely by a robotic goat.

[Beat. The goat keeps bobbing.]

ADHVAITH (V.O.): His title is still under review.

[END - 3:00]

## Shot checklist

1. Home page with Stitch intro card (hold 3s minimum, he bobs)
2. Login page, Google button
3. Wordle: one wrong-word rejection shake, one full solve, today's board
4. Bespokle: full BES stamp reveal with confetti and sound up
5. Trivia: timer bar draining, one reveal with the explanation card
6. BE(spoke)REAL: feed scroll, heart tap animation
7. Two Truths One Lie: statement select, Vote for statement button, locked-in card
8. Story chain scroll
9. Cards page with silhouettes
10. Weekly Culture Digest: photo feature and medal table
11. Leaderboard with Stitch's commentary line visible

## Timing notes

- Total spoken words ~430, comfortable at conversational pace for 3:00
- The two silent beats (Bespokle reveal, final goat hold) buy breathing room
- If running long, cut the login section first, then trim the digest line
